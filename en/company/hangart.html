Put that Art on your wall!
Hangart is the service that provides affordable art prints, the ones that you couldn't get in the original, to the mass. The main goal was to buid a website with simple and pure design, so that users could find the right masterpiece and easily order it's reproduction.
technologies
The technologies are the modern ones, of course.
Django, for developing fast and with vision to scale. Celery for not annoying managers with huge response time. Flickr API for storing images in the safe and free place. jQuery for writing less and doing more. HTML5 and CSS3 for making the pages look as good as they are. And etc.
product page
As you can see, there is absolutely nothing confusing on that page. And at the same time it's quite complex part of site. For client's satisfying custom order was build, with choosing the right size of painting and mat. With every change, the overall picture of final order adjusts to it and also with the price, which gives the client the power to choose depending on the size or money.
hangart journal
Everybody loves to read, and articles about the art refine our sense of beauty. So the Jounal was the place, to say some good words about the art, give a link to paintings that are presented on site and also which related to the article and enhace SEO.
Recommender system
To increase the love and satisfaction of the audience recommender system was created. For collecting the people's ratings there was some sort of quiz, which will give you 5% discount after you rate 15 painting. After data was collected it proceeded through methods of Collaborative filtering. After all the results are presented via "You may also like" on every product page.
